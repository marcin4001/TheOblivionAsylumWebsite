Devlog Beta 1.0 (25 December 2019)

The Oblivion Asylum has entered the development beta phase. The first version of Beta 1.0 is now available. Here is the list of changes:

Added:

  • last final level - Underground laboratory,
  • fight against SCP and new story missions,
  • a simple system of dialogues with Dr. Golimberg,
  • working terminal with information about SCP objects,
  • laboratory door opening system by remote control,
  • USA flag at level 7,
  • microscope in a laboratory at level 5,
  • new models: microscope, laboratory fridge, retro PC, server, gas buttons, door remote control, tables, chair, basket and cupboards from the laboratory, Ray-X Viewer, door button, SCP billboard SCP cards, SCP labels, flag USA, books

Changed:

  • the way to illuminate the key to the main basement on level 3. The key is illuminated when you open the cabinet door,
  • way information appears for 2 missions on the 3rd level. Information appears after interacting with the appropriate door,
  • model of books on shelves for a model with better texture resolution,

I invite you to leave a donation and to download the game.
