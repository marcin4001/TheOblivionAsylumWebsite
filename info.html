Game information

Run, kill them or die

The game is from the survival-horror category. The main target of the game is to get out of the psychiatric hospital where there are zombies that will be on our way to freedom. You will be able to defend yourself against your enemies with a pistol that you can get at the beginning of the game. The current version of the game (Beta 1.0) contains 9 levels, announced artificial intelligence of enemies, playable character, autosave after each level and option menu.

Tip: Zombies chase the player only when zombie see player. You can hide behind the screen of the hospital, in the cabin toilets, or behind the cupboards.

Default control

  • Arrow Up, W – Forward
  • Arrow Down, S – Backwards
  • Arrow Left, A – Left
  • Arrow Right, D – Right
  • Move Mouse - Look around
  • Key E – Action
  • Key X – Hiding and pulling up the pistol
  • Key R – Reload
  • Key F – Flashlight
  • Left mouse button (LMB) – Attack/Shot
  • Key Esc – Pause Menu
  • Key TAB - Inventory
  • Key 1 or 2 - change weapon
  • Key N - Missions Notes
